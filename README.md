# Real Estate Market Analysis

## Project Description
This project analyzes residential real estate listings in Saint Petersburg and the Leningrad region.

The goal was to identify the most attractive market segments and understand seasonal trends to support business strategy for a real estate agency entering the region.

## Business Goal
The client needed to:
- identify high-demand property segments,
- understand factors affecting listing duration,
- analyze seasonal activity in the market,
- determine optimal timing for entering the market.

## Key Analysis Areas

### 1. Listing Activity
- distribution of listings by exposure time
- relationship between property characteristics and sale speed

### 2. Seasonality
- monthly publication activity
- monthly sales activity
- price and area trends by month

## Key Insights

### Market Segments
- The most liquid segment: 2-room apartments (~50–55 m²)
- Smaller apartments sell faster in both regions
- Higher price per m² slows down sales

### Regional Differences
- Saint Petersburg has significantly higher price per m²
- Listings volume in Saint Petersburg is ~5x higher than in the region
- Environmental factors (parks, ponds, airport proximity) affect sales differently across regions

### Seasonality
- Highest activity in autumn (especially October)
- Publication and sales peaks overlap
- Price peaks:
  - listing: August–September
  - sale: March

## Business Value
This analysis helps:
- identify high-demand property segments
- optimize pricing strategy
- choose optimal timing for listings
- plan market entry strategy
