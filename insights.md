## Listing Activity

## Regional Differences

- Saint Petersburg:
  - faster sales for smaller and cheaper properties
- Leningrad region:
  - higher price per m² can still be competitive for smaller apartments :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}  

- Environmental factors:
  - airport proximity negatively impacts sales in the region but not in SPb
  - parks and ponds influence demand differently across regions :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}  

## Seasonality

- Peak activity occurs in autumn (especially October) :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}  
- Publication and sales peaks overlap :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}  

- Price dynamics:
  - listing peak: August–September  
  - sale peak: March :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}  

- Larger apartments are listed more often in autumn but sold more in winter :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}  

## Final Recommendation

The most attractive segment:
- 2-room apartments
- ~50–55 m²
- 1 balcony

Optimal market entry timing:
- October (highest activity)
